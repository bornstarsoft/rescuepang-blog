The upcoming pixel art block-pushing puzzle game, Hazard Pay, is set to launch this June for PlayStation 5, Switch, and PC, and it promises to deliver a unique blend of stealth, survival horror, and narrative choice. Players will step into the shoes of a government cleanup specialist navigating a sinister Cold War-era research facility, where every decision could lead to life or death. The games handcrafted puzzle levels and branching narrative choices are designed to keep players engaged and on edge.

Set against a backdrop of a catastrophic containment breach, Hazard Pay challenges players to erase evidence and uncover the dark secrets hidden within the Institut fr Proteintherapie. With terrifying bio-organisms lurking around every corner, the tension is palpable as players must avoid deadly encounters while deciding the fate of trapped scientists. This game not only tests your puzzle-solving skills but also your moral compass.

As we await the launch, its exciting to think about how Hazard Pay will redefine the puzzle genre with its intricate storytelling and immersive gameplay. Will you be ready to face the horrors within?
