In a significant move for the gaming industry, Shift Up, the developer behind Stellar Blade, has acquired Unbound, the new studio founded by Resident Evil creator Shinji Mikami. This acquisition, announced on Wednesday, marks a pivotal moment as it combines the creative forces of two prominent entities in the gaming world, setting the stage for innovative game development.

Unbound, which was established in 2023, has been quietly working on an original IP, boasting a team of around 50 talented developers. The partnership between Mikami and Shift Up's CEO, Hyung Tae Kim, stems from a shared vision for high-quality game creation, emphasizing the importance of strong creative direction alongside ambitious projects. This collaboration promises to enhance the gaming experience for fans worldwide, with both companies committed to delivering engaging titles.

As we look ahead, the teaser video accompanying the acquisition hints at exciting new projects, including a glimpse of Mikami's next game featuring a multi-winged monster.
