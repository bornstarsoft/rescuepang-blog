Gamirror Games is set to launch AiliA, a groundbreaking visual mirror-based puzzle game, on January 9, 2026. This innovative title, developed by IceToad Studio LLC, invites players to step into a captivating 3D world where reality intertwines with its mirrored counterpart. As players guide Aria, a young girl on a quest to rescue her twin sister, they will manipulate mirrors to reshape their environment and solve intricate puzzles.

What sets AiliA apart is its unique gameplay mechanics that allow players to walk on reflected pathways and interact with the mirrored world as if it were real. With over 100 meticulously crafted puzzles and seven visually stunning stages inspired by Eastern aesthetics, AiliA promises to challenge players' spatial awareness and creative thinking. For those eager to dive deeper, hidden levels offer additional challenges, making it a game that rewards exploration and mastery.

A playable demo is currently available on Steam, allowing players to experience the magic of AiliA before its official release. Will you be ready to embark on this reflective adventure when it launches?
