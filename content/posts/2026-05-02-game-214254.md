If you're looking for a delightful way to unwind this weekend, look no further than the free demo of Nested, an enchanting puzzle game that promises a whimsical experience. With its vibrant aesthetics and engaging gameplay, Nested invites players to solve intricate puzzles by stacking nesting dolls in the right combinations. This innovative approach to puzzle-solving is not only visually appealing but also challenges your strategic thinking.

Developed by Nathan Sioui as part of the GMTK 2024 Game Jam, Nested stands out as one of the top winners, showcasing its potential to become a beloved title in the cozy gaming genre. Players will navigate through various levels, utilizing unique mechanics like color-matching and size interactions, making each puzzle a refreshing challenge. As you explore the game, you'll encounter buttons that can alter your stack's properties, adding layers of complexity and fun.

With the full game set to release in August 2026, now is the perfect time to dive into the demo and experience the charm of Nested. Will you be ready to tackle the puzzles and immerse yourself in this colorful world?
