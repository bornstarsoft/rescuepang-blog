SEGA is gearing up for a remarkable 2026 as it celebrates not just Sonic the Hedgehog's 35th anniversary, but also the beloved puzzle game Puyo Puyo. The company has unveiled the "Dairensa!" project, a tribute to the iconic series, promising fans a plethora of exciting events and initiatives to commemorate this milestone.

Among the highlights is a special live stream scheduled for February 1, 2026, featuring voice actors and exclusive game demos. Additionally, fans in Japan can look forward to pop-up stores and collaborative events that will bring the Puyo Puyo experience to life in vibrant new ways. With the promise of more announcements on the horizon, it's clear that SEGA is committed to making this anniversary unforgettable.
