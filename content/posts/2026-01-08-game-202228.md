The newly launched game AiliA from IceToad Studio LLC is captivating players with its unique mirror-based gameplay mechanics. Available on Steam for $11.99, AiliA invites players to step into a visually stunning world where reality and reflection intertwine. With a 20% launch discount for the first two weeks, its an excellent opportunity for puzzle enthusiasts to dive into this innovative experience.

In AiliA, players control a young girl named Aria as she navigates through various stages that blend the real and mirrored worlds. The game challenges players to manipulate mirrors not just as reflections but as interactive elements that alter the environment and unlock new paths. With over 100 puzzles spread across seven distinct themed areas, AiliA promises a rich and engaging experience for fans of the genre.

For those eager to explore before purchasing, a free demo is available on Steam. The game also features a special collaboration bundle with Moncage, offering an additional discount, making it a perfect time for puzzle lovers to expand their collection. Will you unravel the mysteries hidden within the reflections of AiliA?
