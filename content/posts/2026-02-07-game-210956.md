Amazon Prime subscribers are in for a treat this February, with a selection of exciting free PC games available through Amazon Luna. Notably, the lineup features the much-anticipated Tiny Tina's Wonderlands and the classic strategy game Total War: Attila, both of which can be claimed and kept by subscribers. Unlike other gaming services that restrict access to titles as long as the subscription is active, Amazon Prime allows players to redeem these games via codes, ensuring continued access even after cancellation.

This months offerings include a diverse range of titles, with games available from various digital stores like the Epic Games Store and GOG. Players can look forward to claiming games like Dread Templar and Ambition: A Minuet in Power, among others, throughout the month. Additionally, Amazon Luna continues to expand its cloud gaming service, adding new titles like Alan Wake 2 and Disney Universe, enhancing the gaming experience for subscribers.
