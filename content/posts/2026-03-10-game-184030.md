If youre a cat lover looking for a new gaming experience, then Kitty Haven might just be the perfect fit for you. This upcoming cozy puzzle game challenges players to create a sanctuary for their furry friends, each with unique personalities and needs. The game invites you to arrange spaces that cater to picky little furballs, whether theyre greedy, have sensitive noses, or are prone to stubborn cat fights. 

Unlock items and new residents as you solve puzzles and decorate the ideal haven for your kittens. Available on PC with a demo already out on Steam, Kitty Haven promises to deliver a delightful mix of creativity and strategy. As the gaming landscape continues to evolve, its exciting to see how indie developers are crafting experiences that resonate with niche audiences, especially those who cherish the companionship of pets. 

Will you be diving into the world of Kitty Haven and creating the ultimate cat sanctuary? The future of cozy gaming looks bright, and this title is certainly one to watch!
