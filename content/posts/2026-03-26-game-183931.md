In an exciting collaboration, Hyperkin has partnered with ModRetro to launch color-matched premium controllers for the upcoming M64 gaming console. The new Captain Premium Controllers are designed with retro gamers in mind, featuring a robust build and a comfortable grip that can withstand even the most intense gaming sessions. With a generous 3-meter cable, players can enjoy their gaming experience without being tethered too closely to the console.

These controllers not only offer durability but also come equipped with an extension port for memory cards and rumble feedback accessories, enhancing the overall gaming experience. Hyperkin's Lead Product Developer, Slade Dude Suzuki, expressed enthusiasm for the partnership, emphasizing the community's positive feedback on the Captain Controller and its compatibility with the innovative M64 console.

Set to be released later this year, the new color optionsGreen, Clear, Purple, and Redwill join the existing palette, making it a vibrant addition to any gamer's collection. As we look forward to the launch, one can't help but wonder how these new controllers will elevate the retro gaming experience for both new players and nostalgic veterans alike.
