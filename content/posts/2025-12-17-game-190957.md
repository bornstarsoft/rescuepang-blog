November 2025 proved to be a monumental month for anime-style RPGs in Chinas livestreaming scene, with titles like Kuro Games *Wuthering Waves* and miHoYos *Zenless Zone Zero* capturing millions of viewers. The surge in viewership can be attributed to major version updates and anniversary events that not only kept existing players engaged but also attracted new audiences. This highlights the growing influence of livestreaming platforms, particularly Bilibili, in shaping the gaming landscape.

*Wuthering Waves* achieved an impressive 36.5 million viewers, marking a 103% month-over-month increase, while *Zenless Zone Zero* followed closely with 30.9 million viewers, up 84% from the previous month. These spikes in viewership underscore the importance of strategic content releases and community engagement in the gaming industry. Meanwhile, Yostars *Stella Sora* experienced a staggering 1,721% increase in viewership, showcasing the potential for new titles to break into the mainstream with the right updates.
