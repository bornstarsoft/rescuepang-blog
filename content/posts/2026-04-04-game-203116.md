Get ready for a wild ride as the highly anticipated urban survival game, **Good Luck**, is set to launch on April 8, 2026, for PC, with plans for PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X|S to follow. Developed by the innovative Turkish studio Happybara Games, this physics-based platformer promises a chaotic blend of humor and challenge as players navigate a city filled with absurd traps and unexpected hazards.

In **Good Luck**, players must reach a meeting while dodging everything from banana peels to trash cans, all while battling unpredictable physics and the absence of checkpoints. With both single-player and online co-op modes for up to five players, the game emphasizes teamwork and strategy amidst the chaos. The creators have described it as a unique fusion of urban survival and the rage game subgenre, making it a must-try for fans of frantic multiplayer experiences.

As we count down to the release, one can't help but wonder how this game will redefine the urban survival genre. Will you be ready to tackle the challenges of the urban jungle with friends?
