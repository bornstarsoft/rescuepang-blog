Phantom Feline, the charming 2D puzzle game inspired by classic Game Boy titles, has just received a significant 2.0 update that quadruples its contentall for free! Developed by Shady Paw and published by the UMBC Game Developers Club, this game invites players to step into the paws of Shady the Ghost Cat, who can traverse light in unique ways, albeit without the ability to jump. This update not only enhances gameplay but also expands the universe of Phantom Feline, making it a must-try for puzzle enthusiasts.

The 2.0 update introduces a plethora of exciting features, including 24 new levels, three post-game challenge modes, and Steam Cloud support. Players can now enjoy a more immersive experience with new gimmicks like conveyor belts and pressure pads, as well as enhanced visuals with new particle effects. The game is also now verified for Steam Deck and supports Linux, ensuring that a wider audience can enjoy this delightful puzzle adventure.
