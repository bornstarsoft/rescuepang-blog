If you're a PlayStation Plus subscriber, there's a hidden gem you shouldn't miss: The Exit 8. This free puzzle game, recently added to the PS Plus Extra and Premium tiers, has garnered attention for its unique blend of horror and puzzle-solving elements, making it a must-try for fans of indie games.

Developed by Kotake Create, The Exit 8 immerses players in a first-person experience set in a hauntingly atmospheric Japanese metro tunnel. The gameplay revolves around identifying various anomalies that range from harmless discrepancies to genuinely creepy occurrences. Successfully spotting these anomalies while navigating through the games loops is key to escaping, and its this tension that keeps players engaged.

Though its not a full-length horror game, The Exit 8 offers a captivating experience that can be completed in just a couple of hours. With easy trophies up for grabs, its perfect for those looking for a quick yet thrilling gaming session. As one player noted, its the kind of game you wouldnt typically seek out, but once you play it, youll be glad you did. Will you dare to step into the eerie world of The Exit 8?
